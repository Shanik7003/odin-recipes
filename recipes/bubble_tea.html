Strawberry Bubble Tea

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup black tapioca pearls
  • 2 cups water (for cooking pearls)
  • 1 cup strong black tea, cooled
  • 1/2 cup strawberry puree
  • 1/2 cup milk or non-dairy milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Cook tapioca pearls according to package instructions. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a shaker or large glass, combine black tea, strawberry puree, milk, and sugar.
  3. Add ice and shake or stir well.
  4. Place tapioca pearls at the bottom of a serving glass.
  5. Pour the strawberry milk tea over the pearls.
  6. Serve with a wide straw and enjoy!
