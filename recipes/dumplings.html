Chinese Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 pound (225 g) ground pork or chicken
  • 1 cup finely chopped napa cabbage
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and warm water to form a dough. Knead until smooth and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, combine pork (or chicken), cabbage, green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll the dough into a long log and cut into small pieces. Roll each piece into a small round wrapper.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in each wrapper, fold and seal the edges.
  5. Boil dumplings in salted water until they float (about 5-7 minutes). Optionally, pan-fry for a crispy bottom.
  6. Serve with soy sauce or dipping sauce.
